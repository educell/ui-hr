Who We Are

Educell is the leading platform for delivering seamless collaboration and communication tools for those who have a vested interest in positive, student centered outcomes.

The purpose of Educell is to help facilitate students to have access to as many routes as possible to allow for meaningful and successful outcomes. Educell provides a platform that focuses on team communication and supports service providers in the educational setting to increase student engagement and success. By utilizing Educell, teams can help promote academic, social, and emotional development and growth. With the help of Educell, there is nothing a student can't achieve.

We know that in education communication is critical and Educell provides solutions for districts as well as charter schools that increase the efficacy of the extensive amount of supports provided to the children of our communities.

What We Do

Educell provides a variety of educational supports for administrators and teachers, social workers, and both parents and students in a variety of settings via our web based portal.

    Administrator features:
  • Ability to view and manage student demographics
  • Update and manage student biographical and contact information
  • View and manage assigned social workers on a student-by-student basis

    Teacher features:
  • Ability to add notes and updates to the student profile
  • Ability to edit and update the student profile
  • Ability to add a student to a social worker's caseload

    Social Worker features:
  • Ability to add students and view student status
  • List all of the schools that contain students serviced
  • Add student notes and progress monitoring data
  • View notes on a student profile

How We Do It

At Educell, we believe that all successful student outcomes are determined by the participation and input from all team members which includes the students themselves. By facilitating communication and gathering comprehensive data metrics and documenting progress monitoring, teams will be able to assess whether or not a student is making progress toward desired outcomes. If a student is not making progress then the team can reconvene in a formal setting and determine new approaches in an effort to reinforce the desired outcomes. Educell empowers interested parties by allowing them to have a voice and to collaborate with others.
